Fettuccine Alfredo

Home

Description

Fettuccine boiled al dente and enveloped in a lush, thickened sauce of melted butter simmered with heavy cream, Romano and Parmesan cheeses, plus a hint of garlic salt, salt, and pepper for creaminess and savoriness. Quick to prepare in under 30 minutes, it's best enjoyed hot with a sprinkle of extra cheese.

Ingredients

For 6 servings

  • 680grams of dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 355ml of heavy cream
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic salt
  • 75grams of Romano cheese
  • 50grams of Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. Melt butter into cream in a pan over low heat. Add Salt, Pepper, and Garlic salt.
  3. Increase the heat around medium heat and add the grated Romano and Parmesan cheese until it melted and the sauce thickened.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and mix it until it is well mixed.
  5. Enjoy!
