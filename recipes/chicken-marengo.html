Chicken Marengo

Home

Description

Chicken is browned in olive oil and butter, then simmered with sliced onions, a simple warmed tomato sauce (chopped tomatoes seasoned with salt, pepper, and herbs), thinly sliced button mushrooms, and a thickened "Marengo sauce" of white wine, chicken stock water and flour.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chicken, quartered
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • 1 Sliced Onion
  • 2 Tomatoes, roughy chopped
  • Salt, Pepper and Parsley
  • A quarter L of white wine
  • A quarter L of water of chicken stock or chicken broth
  • 1 dsp of flour
  • Several thinly sliced Button mushrooms

Steps

  1. Brown the chicken pieces in a casserole in the olive oil and a little butter.
  2. Slice the onion into rings. Fry in the oil and butter mixture until translucent.
  3. Prepare a tomato sauce with the tomatoes and cooking over a low heat for a few minutes with the salt, pepper and parsley.
  4. Add these ingredients to the chicken.
  5. Make the Marengo sauce with white wine, chicken stock water (or ckichen broth) and flour.
  6. Add this sauce to the chicken and simmer for about 1 hour.
  7. Chop the mushrooms into thin slices and add these to the chicken and cook for another 15 min.
  8. Enjoy!
